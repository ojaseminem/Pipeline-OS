# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of
background, identity, experience level, discipline, employer, or economic status.

Be respectful, critique work rather than people, protect private information, and
make space for artists and developers who are new to open source. Harassment,
threats, discrimination, sustained disruption, and sexualized conduct are not
accepted.

Report conduct issues privately to the repository owners. Maintainers will confirm
receipt within three business days, avoid conflicts of interest, document actions,
and protect reporter privacy. Responses may include clarification, warning,
temporary restriction, or permanent removal. Retaliation is prohibited.
