# Governance

Vantadeck begins as a maintainer-led project with public decision-making.

- Maintainers merge changes, manage releases, moderate community spaces, and publish
  conflicts of interest.
- Public-contract, security-model, governance, or roadmap changes require an RFC.
- Decisions and material funding relationships are recorded publicly.
- No sponsor receives private roadmap control or closed official features.
- Contributors can become reviewers and maintainers through sustained, constructive,
  technically sound participation documented in `docs/CONTRIBUTOR_LADDER.md`.

Governance will be reviewed when at least five independent regular contributors and
three independent maintainers have participated across two stable release cycles.
That review must consider a representative technical/community council and publish
the result as an RFC.

Appeals go to maintainers not involved in the original decision. If all maintainers
are involved, an agreed independent open-source community member will mediate.
