# Contributor Ladder

- **Contributor:** any accepted contribution.
- **Reviewer:** sustained domain expertise and constructive reviews; may approve in a
  documented area.
- **Maintainer:** cross-area judgment, reliable reviews/releases, community conduct,
  and disclosed conflicts; may merge and release.

Promotions are proposed publicly and require consensus of uninvolved maintainers.
Inactive roles may move to emeritus status without removing attribution.
